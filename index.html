What is UCLouvain
ACM Student Chapter?

The UCLouvain ACM Student Chapter is an affiliated association to the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). It primarily aims promoting computer science among both students and professionals. The association organizes events, tutorials, programming contests... Its activities are open to every interested people.
