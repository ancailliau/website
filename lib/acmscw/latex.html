Discover LaTeX — the professional typesetting system

LaTeX is a markup language used to prepare documents which look profesionnals. It's mostly used by mathematicians, scientists, engineers and people from academic world and from industry. That training, divided in two parts, allows you to discover the LaTeX language.

The practical sessions will, as the theoretical course, be given in French by mentors.

  • First part consists in a « theoretical » course presenting the basis of LaTeX. This part introduce you to what is possible to do using LaTeX, in particular we saw how to include graphics, arrays, mathematics, code... You can download the slides here.
  • Second part consists in practical sessions on computer. In this part, you will be in front of a computer and will be asked to produce documents with LaTeX. Several sessions from different levels are organised: from complete beginner to more advanced. The practical sessions are supervised by tutors.
