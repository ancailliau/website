Discover LaTeX — the professional typesetting system

LaTeX is a markup language used to prepare documents which look profesionnals. It's mostly used by mathematicians, scientists, engineers and people from academic world and from industry. That training, divided in two parts, allows you to discover the LaTeX language.

The course and the practical sessions will be given in French by Sébastien Combéfis and will last 2 hours.

  • First part consists in a « theoretical » course presenting the basis of LaTeX. This part will introduce you to what is possible to do using LaTeX, in particular we will see how to include graphics, arrays, mathematics, code... The major part of the presentation will be focused on how to set up the layout of documents.
  • Second part consists in practical sessions on computer. In this part, you will be in front of a computer and will be asked to produce documents with LaTeX. Several sessions from different levels are organised: from complete beginner to more advanced. The practical sessions are supervised by tutors.

Attending the training is free, but in order to help us organizing the event, you're asked to register to the « theoretical » course using the form aside. The registration for the practical sessions will be done at the end of the « theoretical » course.

The « theoretical » course will be given on Monday November 16th, 2009 16:15 in SUD 19.
